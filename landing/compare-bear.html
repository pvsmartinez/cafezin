Cafezin vs Bear

Bear is a beautiful, minimal Markdown notes app. Cafezin is a local-first AI writing workspace for longer projects — with canvas planning, reviewable AI edits, built-in git, and voice capture. Different tools for different jobs.

Updated: March 2026

Short answer: Choose Bear for clean daily notes with great iOS sync. Choose Cafezin when you are working on a longer writing project and want AI assistance, visual planning, and version control — all local.

Where Cafezin is stronger

  • Built-in AI writing assistance with reviewable edit tracking
  • Canvas for visual planning alongside documents
  • Built-in git for full version history
  • Long-form project structure — chapters, sections, slide decks
  • Voice capture for hands-free drafting
  • Works without internet after setup

Where Bear is stronger

  • Beautiful, minimal design optimized for quick note-taking
  • Excellent Apple ecosystem sync across Mac, iPhone, and iPad
  • Hashtag tagging and fast search across linked notes
  • Lighter footprint — ideal for daily capture and reference
  • Strong mobile experience

The real difference

Bear is a note-taking app with great Markdown support. Cafezin is a writing workspace with AI assistance built in. If your work product is a document, a book, a course, or a long-form draft — Cafezin gives you more structure and more help. If you mostly capture short notes and tag them for later, Bear is better suited.

Who should choose what

Choose Cafezin if you write long-form projects, want AI to help draft and revise, and prefer local files you fully own. Choose Bear if you want a clean daily notebook that syncs beautifully across your Apple devices.

Quick fit

Decision summary

Choose Cafezin if

You are writing a book, course, or long-form project and want AI help, canvas planning, and local-first version control.

Choose Bear if

You want a minimal, beautiful notes app with excellent Apple sync for daily capture and reference.

Biggest tradeoff

Cafezin is project-oriented with AI assistance. Bear is note-oriented with beautiful mobile sync.
