FAQ

Questions about Premium

Can I try it before subscribing?

Yes. Download Cafezin for free and use the editor, canvas, and all productivity features without paying. Premium adds the integrated AI workflows on top of the core workspace.

Which payment methods are supported?

International credit cards including Visa, Mastercard, and Amex. Payments are processed securely by Paddle.

Can I cancel at any time?

Yes, with no penalty and no friction. Access the subscription portal from the Premium page on the site or in Cafezin (Settings -> Account), or contact pvsmartinez@gmail.com. After cancellation, your Premium access remains active until the end of the paid period.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes. We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If it is not a fit, request a refund with no questions asked. Details are available in our Refund Policy.

Is my data safe?

Your files stay on your own device, never on Cafezin servers. API keys are encrypted locally; only account-level data such as email and subscription status are stored. See the full Privacy Policy.

Can pricing change?

Active subscribers are protected. The price you subscribe to is the price you keep while your subscription stays active. Any pricing adjustment will be communicated at least 30 days in advance.
