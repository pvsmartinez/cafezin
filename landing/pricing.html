FAQ

Questions about paid plans

Can I try it before subscribing?

Yes. Download Cafezin for free and use the editor, canvas, and all productivity features without paying. Any paid plan adds the integrated AI workflows on top of the core workspace.

Which payment methods are supported?

International credit cards including Visa, Mastercard, and Amex. Payments are processed securely by Paddle.

Can I cancel at any time?

Yes, with no penalty and no friction. Access the subscription portal from the account page on the site or in Cafezin (Settings -> Account), or contact support@pmatz.com. After cancellation, your paid access remains active until the end of the paid period.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes. We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If it is not a fit, request a refund with no questions asked. Details are available in our Refund Policy.

Is my data safe?

Your files stay on your own device, never on Cafezin servers. API keys are encrypted locally; only account-level data such as email and subscription status are stored. See the full Privacy Policy.

What changes between Basic, Standard, and Pro?

All three unlock AI in Cafezin. The main difference is the amount of included managed Cafezin AI usage each month: $2 on Basic, $15 on Standard, and $40 on Pro. BYOK remains available on every paid tier.
