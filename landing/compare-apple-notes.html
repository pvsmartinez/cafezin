Cafezin vs Apple Notes

Apple Notes is free, fast, and already on every Apple device — it is great for quick capture. Cafezin is built for writers who need more: AI writing assistance, Markdown documents, canvas planning, and version control — all stored locally.

Updated: March 2026

Short answer: Apple Notes wins for quick capture and everyday convenience. Cafezin wins for serious writing projects where you need AI help, plain Markdown files, visual planning, and full project structure.

Where Cafezin is stronger

  • Integrated AI writing assistance with reviewable edit tracking
  • Plain Markdown files stored locally — fully portable, no vendor lock-in
  • Canvas for visual planning, slide decks, and chapter outlines
  • Built-in git for version control and history
  • Voice capture for hands-free drafting
  • Structured workspace for long-form projects
  • Works offline after initial setup, completely

Where Apple Notes is stronger

  • Free and already installed on every Apple device
  • Instant sync across all Apple devices via iCloud
  • Fast startup for quick capture on any Apple device
  • Collaboration features for shared notes
  • Deep Apple integration: Siri, handwriting, locked notes

The real difference

Apple Notes is optimized for capture and everyday convenience. If your writing starts in Apple Notes, it often stays there. Cafezin is designed for the moment the work grows into something serious — a book, a course, a long-form project — and you need more structure, AI assistance, and control over your files. They serve different stages of the same workflow.

Who should choose what

Use both. Keep Apple Notes for quick capture, ideas, and anything that belongs in the Apple ecosystem. Use Cafezin when a project deserves its own workspace, AI assistance, and version history.

Quick fit

Decision summary

Choose Cafezin if

You are working on a serious project and want AI review, canvas planning, local Markdown files, and git history.

Use Apple Notes if

You want quick capture, deep Apple sync, and the convenience of a free built-in app for everyday notes.

Do both work together?

Yes. Apple Notes is great for capturing ideas. Cafezin is where serious projects live and grow.
