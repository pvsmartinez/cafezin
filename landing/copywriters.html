Cafezin for Copywriters

From brief to final copy, Cafezin keeps every project in plain Markdown files on your Mac. AI assistance for drafting and editing, a canvas for editorial planning, and built-in git so client revision rounds never get lost.

Updated: March 2026

The problem: Copy lives across scattered docs, email threads, and half-finished Google Docs. Cafezin puts every project in a structured local workspace — brief, research, drafts, and final copy, all versioned and searchable.

Draft faster with AI, approve every change

Give Cafezin's AI a brief and a direction. It drafts. You review every change it made before accepting — no invisible rewrites, no surprises in the final file. Rewrite tone, adjust for audience, generate alternatives, all in one place.

Canvas for editorial calendars and content plans

Map out a month of content on the canvas before writing a single word. Arrange posts by date, drag ideas around, link to the documents where each piece lives. Visual planning and writing in the same workspace.

Project files, not scattered docs

Each client or campaign gets a workspace folder. Markdown files for brief, research notes, drafts, and approved copy. Git keeps every version — you can always go back to the draft the client liked in round two.

Voice capture for on-the-go ideas

Campaign idea in the shower? A hook that hit you on a walk? Record it on your phone via the Cafezin iOS companion and it lands as a draft note in your workspace.

Works offline

Deadline on a flight? Cafezin's AI and all your files run locally. No cloud dependency when you need to ship.

Quick answers

Copywriters ask

Can I share copy drafts with clients?

Cafezin files are plain Markdown. Export to any format (Word, PDF, HTML) via a Markdown converter, or share the .md file directly. Git makes it easy to track which version a client approved.

Does the AI write in my brand voice?

You can prime the AI with brand guidelines and tone examples at the top of a document. It follows the context you set. The more specific the brief, the closer the output to your voice.

What about SEO copy workflows?

Write your keyword list and outline in one Markdown file, draft in another. The AI can follow SEO briefs and generate structured content. All version-controlled for client rounds.
