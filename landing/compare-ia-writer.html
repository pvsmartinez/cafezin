Cafezin vs iA Writer

iA Writer is one of the best focused Markdown editors — clean, opinionated, and built around getting the words down. Cafezin is an AI writing workspace that goes further: it adds reviewable AI assistance, visual planning, built-in git, and voice capture alongside your Markdown documents.

Updated: March 2026

Short answer: Use iA Writer when you want the cleanest possible Markdown experience with no distractions. Use Cafezin when you also need AI assistance, visual planning, and version history integrated into the workflow.

Where Cafezin is stronger

  • AI writing assistance built directly into the editor
  • Reviewable AI edits — see what the AI changed and approve or reject
  • Canvas for visual planning and slide-style layouts
  • Built-in git for transparent version history without third-party tools
  • Voice capture for recording ideas without breaking writing flow
  • Structured workspace for multi-file projects

Where iA Writer is stronger

  • Deeply considered focus experience with minimal chrome
  • Style Check feature that highlights filler words and clichés
  • Author Mode to highlight your own voice within a draft
  • Cross-platform with excellent iOS and Android support
  • Faster startup time for a single-file writing session

The real difference

iA Writer's philosophy is that the best writing experience has as little friction as possible between the writer and the page. Cafezin's philosophy is that the writing process needs more: planning before the draft, AI assistance during it, and visible review after. If your workflow is a single document and a blank page, iA Writer is hard to beat. If the project needs to move through stages, Cafezin is more complete.

Who should choose what

Choose iA Writer if minimalism and focus are your highest priority and AI assistance is not part of your writing process. Choose Cafezin if you want AI help that is reviewable and transparent, plus canvas and git, in a local-first environment.

Quick fit

Decision summary

Choose Cafezin if

You want reviewable AI edits, canvas planning, and local git history alongside a clean Markdown editor.

Choose iA Writer if

You want the most focused, distraction-free Markdown editor with great mobile and cross-platform support.

Biggest tradeoff

Cafezin adds depth: AI, canvas, git. iA Writer adds focus by removing everything except the text.
