About

Built in Brazil.
Made for people who think with text.

Cafezin is an independent project — a local-first AI writing workspace built by one developer and shaped by the people who use it every day.

How it started

Cafezin grew out of a personal frustration. Pedro Martinez, a Brazilian software developer, kept switching between AI chat tools, note apps, and document editors every time he sat down to write. The constant context-switching was getting in the way of the work itself.

The idea was simple: build a single workspace that brings structured writing, an AI assistant, and a visual canvas under the same roof — and keep all files local, where they belong. No cloud lock-in, no subscription that quietly owns your data.

The first version was used by friends. Then by colleagues. Then by people those colleagues recommended it to. The tool improved with every conversation, and that feedback loop is still how Cafezin grows today.

Who it's for

Cafezin is built for professionals who use writing as a core part of their work — writers, educators, students, researchers, and analysts. People who draft, revise, outline, and think in text every day, and who want AI to help without taking over.

It's designed to fit the workflow you already have, not to replace it with a new one.

How it works

Bring your own AI key

Cafezin is model-agnostic. It works with any OpenAI-compatible API — OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, local models via Ollama, or anything else that speaks the same protocol. You connect your own key directly. Cafezin never sees your usage, never marks it up, and never proxies your requests. Your key, your cost, your control.

Your data stays on your machine

Files are stored locally. API keys are kept in your device's local storage, separate from everything else, and are never transmitted to Cafezin servers. That is the entire point of local-first.

Simple, honest pricing

The core product is free. Premium is a small license that unlocks advanced AI workflows. There is no recurring subscription for features that should simply work. What you pay for is clear; what's free stays free.

What comes next

The roadmap is genuinely open. Cafezin is actively evolving, and the people using it today have the most influence over what gets built next. iOS and Android support are in progress. Improvements to the editor, the canvas, and the AI workflows ship with every release.

If you have a feature request, a workflow that doesn't quite fit, or just want to share how you're using Cafezin — please reach out. Feedback from early users directly shapes the tool, and every message is read.
